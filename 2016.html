Meteoric Rise


BTS’ special compilation album The Most beautiful Moment In Life: Young Forever was released on May 2nd and would earn them their first ever Album of the Year award at the Melon Music Awards. After that it was hit after hit: “Young Forever”, “Fire”, “Save Me” and the absolute decadence that was and will always be “Blood, Sweat & Tears”. This Moombahton banger was accompanied by a baroque style music video and was an artistic continuation of the BTS Universe. The song itself simultaneously topped all eight South Korean music charts also known as an “all-kill”. This single was included in their second Korean studio album, Wings released on October 10th. It became their first album to sell one million copies and Rolling Stone named it “one of the most conceptually and sonically ambitious pop albums of 2016”. For the first time, it included solo tracks from each member exploring themes of temptation and adversity.



This was an especially active year for BTS in terms of promoting. The band made various appearances in television shows and web series. Taehyung starred in Flower Boy Bromance with his close actor friend, Minjae and Jungkook would do the same in June with senior idol singer, Minwoo from Shinhwa. As a band, they played a game on Korea’s most famous variety show, Running Man for the 300th episode and they also performed at KCON in LA. In August, BTS attended the Idol Star Athletics Championship” (ISAC), a TV program where idols compete in hopes of winning and spreading their bands' names, but no one really enjoys being there. A year later, they’d break a bunch of rules like “no posting photos of the event before it airs” to ban themselves from ever attending again. Classic BTS.
