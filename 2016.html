Meteoric Rise


BTS’ second Korean studio album, Wings released in October of 2016 became their first album to sell one million copies and became the best selling album in Goan Album Chart history at the time. It included solo tracks from each member exploring themes of temptation and adversity. Rolling Stone named it “one of the most conceptually and sonically ambitious pop albums of 2016”. The accompanying single “Blood, Sweat & Tears'' simultaneously topped all eight South Korean music charts. Many veteran fans found out about them at this time because of the beautifully poetic music video and memorable choreography.




In August, they started Run BTS, a variety series broadcasted on the web where the members play games and complete challenges for rewards or penalties. This resulted in fans making memes of the show and forming friendships with each other through their shared interest. The company wanted BTS to feel relatable rather than out-of-reach stars. This was additionally achieved through livestreams, brief vlogs on Youtube and their Bon Voyage series documenting their yearly vacations together. This content resulted in community building that would prove integral to BTS’ success.








Other Notable Dates

May 5, 2015

“I Need U” won on The Show, marking their first time taking first place on one of South Korea’s weekly music shows. It took them two years since debut.

May 7, 2015

Embarked on their first world tour Episode II: The Red Bullet. They performed outside of Asia at Sydney, Melbourne, New York City, Dallas, Chicago, Los Angeles, Mexico, São Paulo and Chile.

Jun. 04, 2015

Released their first Japanese Single MV, "For You".

Jun. 30, 2015

The single “Dope” hits number three on Billboard's World Digital Chart for the first time.

Oct. 26, 2015

Became brand ambassadors for PUMA. This would be the first of their many brand partnerships. Future endorsements include Fila, Hyundai Motors, LG, Samsung and Coca Cola.

Nov. 30, 2015

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2. debuted on the Billboard 200 for the first time at No. 171.

Nov. 07, 2015

Won their second award at the Melon Music Awards for Best Dance-Male.

Dec. 02, 2015

Won Best World Performer at the MNET Asian Music Awards.
