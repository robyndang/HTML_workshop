Building the Foundation


The last part of BTS’ “school trilogy”, Skool Luv Affair was released in January and topped the Gaon Album chart selling 100,000 copies that year. It was also the first time BTS appeared on the Billboard’s World Albums Chart, peaking at number three. Although it was musically similar to their first two albums, the theme shifted to young love in order to grow with their fanbase. That July, the band members took to Hollywood Boulevard to hand out 200 flyers for their free and first US concert and in August, they released their debut studio album Dark & Wild which peaked at number 3 on the weekly Billboard World Albums chart. Fun fact! The lead single “Danger” was recorded in a Los Angeles garage.
