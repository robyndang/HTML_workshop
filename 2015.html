Mainstream Breakthrough


Up until this point, BTS’ brand was built on aggressive hip hop and displays of masculinity. To express the beauty and angst of “youth” in their next EP, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1, they diversified to pop and EDM and switched up their fashion to match. The music video for “I Need U” marks the beginning of BTS Universe, a coming of age storyline about friendship, love and loss that would continue in future music videos. Fans enjoy dissecting, understanding and connecting the dots of these plot points and discussing them with each other. By creating their own cinematic universe it added more depth and meaning to their songs and kept fans coming back for more. In terms of smart business moves, it was genius because BTS Universe would later go on to become a webtoon and mobile game and is currently in production as a kdrama.




In August, they started Run BTS, a variety series broadcasted on the web where the members play games and complete challenges for rewards or penalties. In the show, they’ve stumbled their way through every skill imaginable: floristry, bowling, cooking, go-kart racing, voice acting and of course, bottle-flipping, just one of the few mundane games BTS manages to make a whole lot of fun. Since its start, the production value has skyrocketed allowing them to rent out entire malls just to go on a scavenger hunt or whole stadiums just to shoot at each other with lasers. What hasn’t changed though is the envy-inducing dynamic of BTS’ friendship. It’s clear to see how much they enjoy each other’s company and it leaves fans, old and new, coming back for more wholesome chaos.








Other Notable Dates

May 5, 2015

“I Need U” won on The Show, marking their first time taking first place on one of South Korea’s weekly music shows. It took them two years since debut.

May 7, 2015

Embarked on their first world tour Episode II: The Red Bullet. They performed outside of Asia at Sydney, Melbourne, New York City, Dallas, Chicago, Los Angeles, Mexico, São Paulo and Chile.

Jun. 04, 2015

Released their first Japanese Single MV, "For You".

Jun. 30, 2015

The single “Dope” hits number three on Billboard's World Digital Chart for the first time.

Oct. 26, 2015

Became brand ambassadors for PUMA. This would be the first of their many brand partnerships. Future endorsements include Fila, Hyundai Motors, LG, Samsung and Coca Cola.

Nov. 30, 2015

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2. debuted on the Billboard 200 for the first time at No. 171.

Nov. 07, 2015

Won their second award at the Melon Music Awards for Best Dance-Male.

Dec. 02, 2015

Won Best World Performer at the MNET Asian Music Awards.
